Who Are We?

MSU Valorant is a club run at Michigan State University for the video game Valorant by game developer Riot Games. Valorant is known in the gaming world as a tactical first person shooter where players must work together in order to win more rounds than their opponents. MSU Valorant is a club for all people at the University and surrounding areas who want to discuss and enjoy their passion for the game. The club hosts multiple events every semester including bi-weekly in person meetings at a local LAN center (a place with many gaming PCs that are usable for playing Valorant) where new friendships are born over the enjoyment of our favorite game Valorant.

Our Mission

MSU Valorant's mission as an affiliated club at MSU is to provide a non-discriminating environment for MSU and the surrounding community to enjoy our favorite video game. MSU Valorant also has the goal of being a premier collegiate esport destination for students looking to be the best esports players at the college level.

How to Participate

If you're looking to be the next collegiate Valorant star and improve your skills or just make new friends with other MSU Valorant players, you can participate by joining the club's public discord and the link can be found below to join! Within the discord, you can assign yourself roles and look for other Valorant players to play with no matter what your rank is! Anyone and everyone is welcome to join as MSU Valorant is an accepting community.
