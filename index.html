Welcome!

Welcome to Michigan State University Valorant Club! We are the premier esports club at Michigan State University for the video game Valorant by Riot Games. Want to meet other Valorant players and hone your skills as a Valorant Player? Well, you've come to the right place!

What Is Valorant?

Valorant is a competitive tactical First Person Shooter developed by game developer Riot Games. The goal in Valorant is to win more rounds than your opponent and the first to win 13 rounds in a match wins. Valorant requires extreme team coordination and mechanical skill to compete at the highest level, but is also fun at a casual level too, making it a game for anyone and everyone. If you would like to learn more about Valorant as a whole, you can visit the Valorant website to learn more, happy fragging!
